Ingredients:
 - 10 chicken wings
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
 - 3/4 cup potato starch
 - grape seed oil for frying

 Sauce ingredients:
 - 1 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 2 tablespoon water
 - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
 - 1/2 corn syrup
 

 Directions:
 1. Wash chicken wings with milk.
 2. Wash them with water.
 3. Wipe off the water and then sprinkle chicken wings with salt and pepper.
 4. Coat chicken wings with potato starch.
 5. Fry chicken wings in oil until starting to brown.
 6. Add sauce ingredients to another pan.
 7. Heat the sauce until it starts to bubble.
 8. Add fried chicken wings to the sauce and mix them.
